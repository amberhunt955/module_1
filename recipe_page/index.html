Morning Magic Milkshake

A milkshake that's sure to wake you up and feel ready to take on the day! Balancing out the ice cream with some healthy choices and a bit of espresso to get you going... you'll love it!

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Ice Cream
  • Chocolate
  • Nuts
  • Spinach
  • Espresso

Method

  1. Pour in milk
  2. Add Ice Cream
  3. Mix in other ingredients
  4. Blend
